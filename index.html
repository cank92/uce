UNICHROME BP

Despite novel alternatives, Chrome tanning is still a cornerstone of the leather tanning process, providing strength, quality and high shrinkage temperatures. UNICHROME BP is an inorganically reduced basic chrome sulfate with low basicity. It is used in the leather industry for tanning and retanning of hides and double-face. UNICHROME BP gives the leather a soft tanning effect. Due to its rapid penetration and high yield, it gives the leather a homogeneous tanning.
